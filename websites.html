FAQ

Can I update the site myself?

Yes. It’s plain HTML/CSS/JS. I’ll include a short guide and keep the code organized for easy edits.

Do you host it?

I configure GitHub Pages (or your preferred host) and point your domain. SSL (HTTPS) is included.

What do you need from me?

Logo/brand colors, text content, and any images. If you don’t have assets, I can help with a simple, clean default.
